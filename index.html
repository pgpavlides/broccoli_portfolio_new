Info About Me

Hello! My name is Polichronis. I am a photographer/videographer and i love image & video editing! I have been into photography as long as i can remember. I studied at Ionian University at the department of Audio and Visual Arts.

Experience

Most of my experience comes from Freelancing. This gave me the opportunity to speak with different kinds of people. That gave me the ability to.

Feeling

For me, photography is a way of expressing the world within me, but also a way of communicating with the world around me. I get excited with the ability to isolate and capture scenes or details of a scene or a natural phenomenon that seem insignificant to other people. I also like editing a simple photo to the fullest, so that the final result is something very different from the original, but at the same time without losing its basic features. This way I can change the way we see the world and also convey a different message.
